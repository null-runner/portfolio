    Safety rules always enforced first

    • R1
      Refunds → always human. Money leaving the business is never decided by the AI alone.
    • R2
      Confidence < 0.75 → human. If the model is unsure, a person confirms.
    • R3
      Angry sentiment → human. Upset customers get a real person, not an autoreply.
    • R4
      Unknown category → fallback. If it cannot classify, it hands off instead of guessing.
    The AI can draft a reply for every case, but a draft is only ever a suggestion. Sending stays a human action.
